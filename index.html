About the Artist

The Creative Mind
Behind the Brush

Hi, I'm Naganath G S — a caricaturist, watercolour painter, cartoonist and web designer based in Karnataka, India. With over a decade of experience, I've dedicated my life to the craft of visual storytelling.

My work spans live event caricatures, detailed watercolour portraits, editorial cartoons, brand identities, comic strips and modern web design. Every project is treated as a unique creative challenge.
